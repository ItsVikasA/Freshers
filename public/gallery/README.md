# Gallery Images

Place your event images in this directory.

## Image Naming Convention

- img1.jpg
- img2.jpg
- img3.jpg
- etc.

## Recommended Image Specifications

- Format: JPG, PNG, or WebP
- Resolution: 1920x1080 or higher
- File size: < 2MB (optimized for web)
- Aspect ratio: 16:9 or 1:1 (square)

## Categories

Organize your images by categories:
- Performances
- Games & Activities
- Food & Refreshments
- Group Photos
- DJ Night
- Awards & Prizes
- Behind the Scenes

Update the `images` array in `src/components/Gallery.tsx` with your actual image paths and categories.
